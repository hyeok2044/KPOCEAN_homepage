Welcome to KP OCEAN

KP OCEAN is a rapidly growing company with a variety of ship types. As such, opportunities for promotion are open to you, and we will manage your CAREER and provide a quick path to promotion to captain and chief engineer.

Why Join KPOCEAN?

• Variety of ship types

• Many opportunities for promotion and career development
(Rating to Officer, Master, C/E)

• Free internet for all fleets

• Rejoining bonus

• Quick and friendly staff response

• No delay of salary

• Cash advance


Application and approval procedures

• Submit your application, then send an email to
(recruiting@kp-ocn.com).

• The Crew Manager will contact you for an interview via mobile phone or Facebook video call.

• If approved, submit the original documents and undergo a medical test. (Master, C/E, C/O, 1AE may require a principal interview)

• Training (KML, ECDIS, required principles).

• In-house training and dispatch.
